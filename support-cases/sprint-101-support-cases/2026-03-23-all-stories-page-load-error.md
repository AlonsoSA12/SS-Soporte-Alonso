# All Stories Page Load Error

## Date
2026-03-23

## Context

While reviewing the platform, the `All Stories` page failed to load and returned a `Something Went Wrong` error.

Other modules were still working correctly, which suggested that the problem was isolated to this section or its underlying endpoint.

---

## Actions Taken

- Accessed the `All Stories` section to reproduce the issue
- Confirmed that the page failed after the loading attempt
- Verified that other platform modules continued to respond normally
- Registered the case as a high-severity functional issue in QA

---

## Result

The incident was recorded in the QA platform as a blocking issue affecting the `All Stories` module.

---

0.5 hours
