# Post QA Approval Story Points Mismatch

## Date
2026-03-23

## Context

A discrepancy was detected between the total shown in the `Post QA Approval Stories` summary card and the actual Story Points visible in the `Finished After QA` column.

This inconsistency affected reporting reliability and made the dashboard totals less trustworthy for project tracking.

---

## Actions Taken

- Reviewed the `Post QA Approval Stories` summary card
- Compared the displayed total against the visible Story Points in the corresponding column
- Documented the mismatch between the summary value and the board value
- Registered the case in QA for later correction

---

## Result

The inconsistency was documented in the QA platform, leaving clear traceability of the mismatch between the summary card and the board data.

---
